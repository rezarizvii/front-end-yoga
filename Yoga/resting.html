Resting & Supine Poses

Happy Baby Pose

Ananda Balasana

Happy Baby Pose gently stretches the inner groins and the back spine. It also calms the brain and helps relieve stress and fatigue.

Child's Pose

Balasana

Child’s Pose gently stretches the hips, thighs, and ankles. It relieves back and neck pain when done with head and torso supported. Balasana also calms the brain and helps relieve stress and fatigue.

Corpse Pose

Savasana

Savasana calms the brain and helps relieve stress and mild depression. It also relaxes the body, reduces headache, fatigue, and insomnia, and helps to lower blood pressure.

Wide Forward Bend Pose

Upavistha Konasana

Wide-Angled Seated Forward Bend lengthens the hamstrings and stretches the back, thighs and calves. It can increase mental calmness and decrease anxiety.

One-Legged Pigeon Pose

Eka Pada Rajakapotasana

This pose stretches your thighs, inner hips, and buttocks in different ways in your bent and straight legs.

Reclining Angle Pose

Supta Baddha Konasana

It stimulates abdominal organs like the ovaries and prostate gland, bladder, kidneys and the heart and improves general circulation. It apso stretches the inner thighs, groins, and knees. It can relieve the symptoms of stress, mild depression, menstruation and menopause
